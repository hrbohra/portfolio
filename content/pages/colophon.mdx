<Plain>
Every claim this site makes about how I work is checkable against the site itself. It is
written twice, top to bottom: the toggle in the corner switches every page between the
version for people who read stories and the version for people who read logs. It loads
fast because it ships almost nothing. And it was built the way the AI page describes:
plans first, AI-assisted, verified, written up.
</Plain>
<Eng>
Next.js App Router, fully static export. No database, no server state, no forms. Content
is MDX with Zod-validated frontmatter; the two voices are paired semantic components, both
prerendered into the static HTML, swapped by a root data attribute with zero layout shift.
Facts and metrics come from a single typed constants module; site copy may not retype a
number. Media lives on Cloudflare R2 behind its own subdomain, except the small receipts
on this page, which ship with the site. Hosted on Vercel's free tier.
</Eng>

## Receipts

<Plain>
Not descriptions of quality: measurements of it, taken against the live site, with the
full reports linked.
</Plain>
<Eng>
Captured 17 July 2026 against production. Lighthouse 12.8.2, emulated mobile, default
throttling. CI asserts floors on every push; the numbers below are the live-site runs.
</Eng>

**Lighthouse, live site, home page:** performance 98 · accessibility 98 · best practices
96 · SEO 100. Largest contentful paint 1.9s, layout shift 0, total blocking time 150ms.
[Full report](/receipts/lighthouse-home.report.html) ·
[CI pipeline](https://github.com/hrbohra/portfolio/actions) ·
[the workflow file](https://github.com/hrbohra/portfolio/blob/main/.github/workflows/ci.yml)

**First-load JavaScript:** 103 to 106 kB on every page, against a 120 kB budget checked at
build.

**The rules are executable:** an editorial lint (banned punctuation and vocabulary,
confidentiality tags) and a metrics guard (every number on this site must exist in the
canonical facts module) fail the build on violation.
[Architecture decision records](https://github.com/hrbohra/portfolio/tree/main/docs/adr),
including the rejected options and one honest deviation.

**Gates catching their own author:**
[this failed run](https://github.com/hrbohra/portfolio/actions/runs/29580465065) is the
home redesign scoring 0.65 on performance because the hero was wrapped in a scroll reveal
and rendered invisible until hydration. The fix shipped 40 minutes later and the
[next run](https://github.com/hrbohra/portfolio/actions) is green. A wall of unbroken
green would mean the gates test nothing.
