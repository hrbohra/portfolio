<Plain>
Between client projects I ran myself through a curriculum nobody set: making AI models work
on my own machine, in every shape that matters. Chatbots that show their reasoning step by
step. Systems that answer questions from a folder of PDF documents with citations. Small
models served locally with no cloud in sight, and even early text-to-video generation. I
also automate my own life: a tool that gathers and scores job listings from several sources
overnight.
</Plain>
<Eng>
An o1-style chain-of-thought chatbot on TinyLlama 1.1B. Prompt-engineering comparison
pipelines. A Dockerized RAG chatbot over PDF corpora. A Dockerized book-analysis bot on
Hugging Face Transformers. Local Llama 3.2 serving via llama-stack, vLLM-style Llama
inference, Deepseek exploration, Text2Video-Zero, and Rasa NLU dialogue systems. Plus a
Puppeteer-driven multi-source job aggregator with weighted scoring. The point of the year
was calibration: knowing first-hand what each tool can and cannot do, which is the
foundation of the AI page on this site.
</Eng>
