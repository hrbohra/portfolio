<Plain>
Two small, honest data projects. A residential energy analyser that reads consumption data,
checks it rigorously before trusting it, models usage, and presents the findings in an
interactive dashboard. And for a sports-analytics client: gathering football statistics from
the major public sources and training a model that predicts player ratings from performance
numbers.
</Plain>
<Eng>
Energy analyser: pandas ingestion with Pydantic validation, statistical analysis, feature
engineering, a scikit-learn RandomForest model, an interactive Plotly dashboard, and pytest
coverage over the pipeline. Sports work: football statistics collection in R (worldfootballR
over FBref, Transfermarkt and Understat) and Python, feeding a RandomForestRegressor with
feature selection, scaling and honest evaluation.
</Eng>
